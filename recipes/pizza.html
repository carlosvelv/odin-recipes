Description

This recipe guides you through creating a classic homemade pizza from scratch. It features a simple yeast dough that rises to create a perfectly chewy and airy crust. The base is topped with a bright, savory tomato sauce seasoned with garlic and herbs, which is then covered with melted mozzarella and your choice of toppings. The pizza is baked at a high temperature until the crust is golden-brown and the cheese is bubbly and delicious.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose or "00" Flour
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Warm Water
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar (optional, to help activate yeast)
  • Canned Whole or Crushed Tomatoes (San Marzano recommended)
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Dried Oregano or Fresh Basil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Pepperoni
  • Low-Moisture Mozzarella Cheese (shredded or fresh)

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast in warm water, optionally with a pinch of sugar.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour.
  4. Mix until a shaggy dough forms, then knead on a floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  6. Gently punch down the risen dough and divide it as needed.
  7. While the dough rises, prepare your pizza sauce (e.g., blend canned tomatoes with garlic, oregano, and salt).
  8. Shred your low-moisture mozzarella cheese.
  9. Slice your pepperoni.
  10. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (e.g., 500°F or 260°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  11. On a floured surface, stretch or roll your dough into the desired shape.
  12. Transfer the shaped dough to a pizza peel or parchment paper.
  13. Spread a thin, even layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  14. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  15. Arrange the pepperoni slices on top of the cheese.
  16. Carefully slide the pizza (on the parchment or from the peel) onto the hot stone or baking sheet.
  17. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden-brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  18. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
